A FIELD-AWARE FORK

THE CALCULATOR FOR PEOPLE WHO FORGOT THEY ALREADY PLAYED CARDS

A modified version of Solumin's Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories Fusion Calculator, expanded by Fortefyre to include cards already sitting on the field.

WHERE IT CAME FROM

THE ORIGINAL PROJECT

01

Solumin Built the Calculator

This website is a modified fork of the YGO-FM Fusion Calculator, originally created by Solumin.

Solumin created the original interface, fusion logic, and project structure. Fortefyre did not create the original calculator.

02

The Idea Came From a Let's Play

The original project was inspired by Giver336's Something Awful playthrough of Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories.

Co-commentator General Yeti wondered whether a program could find useful fusions automatically. Solumin answered that question by building one.

THE FIELD EXPERIMENT

WHAT FORTEFYRE CHANGED

“He's bad at the game, and wanted to include the field cards.”

Five hand slots The original calculator's standard hand support.
Five field slots Cards already in play can now be entered separately.
Hand + field checks The calculator checks whether a hand card can fuse with or equip a field card.
Clear attribution The databases, original design, and underlying project remain credited to their original creators and contributors.
